Grilled Salmon Bowl with Avocado
My go-to weeknight dinner that never disappoints. Fresh wild salmon, perfectly ripe avocado, pickled radish, and a drizzle of homemade sesame-ginger dressing. Takes about 25 minutes from start to finish.
March 6, 2026 at 7:32 AM
Ingredients Instructions
  • 2 fillets wild salmon
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 2 tbsp sesame-ginger dressing
  • 1/2 cup pickled radish
