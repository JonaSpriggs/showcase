Mediterranean Grain Bowl

by Chef Sarah Mitchell
10 min prep
25 min cook
2 servings
Utensils
Large Skillet Mixing Bowl Whisk Baking Sheet
Servings
2
Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1/3 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp fresh herbs (parsley, mint)
1
Cook quinoa according to package directions. Fluff with a fork and let cool to room temperature.
15 min Prep ahead
2
Prepare the vegetables: halve the cherry tomatoes, dice the cucumber, and slice the olives.
5 min
3
In a large bowl, combine the cooled quinoa with all the prepared vegetables. Add crumbled feta cheese.
3 min
4
Whisk together olive oil and lemon juice. Pour over the bowl and toss gently. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve.
2 min
